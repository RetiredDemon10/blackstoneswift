Frequently Asked

Got Questions?

We have answers. If you don't find what you need, our support team is available 24/7.

Is Blackstone Swift FDIC insured?
Yes. All deposits are FDIC insured up to $250,000 per depositor, giving you federal protection on every dollar you hold with us.
How quickly can I open an account?
Most accounts are approved in under 3 minutes. Just fill in your details, verify your identity, and you're ready to go.
Are there any hidden fees?
Absolutely none. No monthly maintenance fees, no minimum balance requirements, and no fees on standard transfers. Ever.
Can I use Blackstone Swift internationally?
Yes. Your debit card works at any ATM worldwide for free, and you can send money to 150+ countries at real exchange rates.
What's the savings APY?
Our High-Yield Savings account currently offers 4.80% APY, reviewed and updated weekly to ensure you always get a competitive rate.
How do I contact support?
We're available 24/7 via live chat in the app, email, and phone. Average first-response time is under 90 seconds.
